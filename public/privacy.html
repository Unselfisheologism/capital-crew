Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Capital Crew ("we", "us", "the game") is an economic strategy game operated as a personal project. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

DataPurposeStorage
UsernameAccount identificationSupabase database (cloud)
Password (bcrypt hash)AuthenticationSupabase database (cloud)
Game session dataMultiplayer state syncSupabase Realtime (cloud, ephemeral)
Server settingsGame server configurationSupabase database (cloud)

We do not collect: real names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, browsing history, or any advertising/tracking data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Account creation & login — Your username and hashed password are used solely to authenticate you and identify your game session.
  • Multiplayer gameplay — Your username and in-game actions are shared with other players in your game server for real-time multiplayer synchronization.
  • Server management — If you create a game server, your username is stored as the host identity for that server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is processed exclusively through Supabase, our hosting and database provider. Supabase processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy and SOC 2 compliance standards.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained until you request deletion.
  • Game session data — Ephemeral; discarded when the server session ends.
  • Temporary servers — Automatically deleted after the configured expiry period.

6. Data Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (10 rounds) before transmission. We never store or transmit plaintext passwords. All API communication occurs over HTTPS. Database access is restricted via row-level security policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Capital Crew is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, do not create an account.

8. Your Rights

  • Access — You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact: privacy@capital-crew.pages.dev
